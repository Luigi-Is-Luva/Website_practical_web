About Us

Welcome to Luigi's , where authentic Italian flavors meet modern elegance. Founded in 2010 by Chef Luigi Dominguez, our restaurant has been a beloved destination for food lovers seeking genuine Italian cuisine.


Our story began when Chef Marco left his family's restaurant in Naples to bring traditional Italian recipes to America. Every dish is prepared using time-honored techniques passed down through generations, combined with the finest locally-sourced ingredients.


At Luigi's , we believe that food is more than sustenance—it's an experience that brings people together. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual dinner with friends, our warm atmosphere and exceptional service will make you feel like part of our family.
